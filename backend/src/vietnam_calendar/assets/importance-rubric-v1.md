# Importance rubric v1

Apply relevance first. Then assess certainty, geographic/population impact, institutional or economic consequence, and historical significance. Confirmed decisions outrank plans. `must_include` is independent from the four-level importance scale and covers coups, mid-term top-leader resignations, armed interstate conflict, very large political collective action, large anti-government demonstrations, and formal central-bank policy-rate changes. All outputs require human review in Phase 3.
